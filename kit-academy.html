ABOUT KIT ACADEMY

Kit Academy is a premier youth soccer development program dedicated to nurturing young athletes ages U6 through U10. Our mission is to provide a foundation of technical skills, tactical understanding, and physical development while fostering a lifelong passion for soccer.

We believe in developing the complete player - not just on the field, but also building character, sportsmanship, and teamwork that extends beyond the game.

TIERED TRAINING

Tailored training programs designed for each age group to maximize development and enjoyment.

U6

LITTLE KICKERS

Introduction to soccer through fun games and activities that develop basic motor skills and ball familiarity.

  • 45-minute sessions
  • 1:8 coach to player ratio
  • Focus on coordination
  • Non-competitive environment
U8

RISING STARS

Building fundamental soccer skills while introducing simple tactics and team concepts in a positive environment.

  • 60-minute sessions
  • 1:10 coach to player ratio
  • Technical skill development
  • Small-sided games
U10

FUTURE CHAMPIONS

Advanced training to prepare players for competitive soccer with emphasis on technique, tactics, and game understanding.

  • 75-minute sessions
  • 1:12 coach to player ratio
  • Position-specific training
  • Introduction to competition
