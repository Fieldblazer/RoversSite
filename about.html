About Texarkana Rovers FC

Discover our passion, history, and commitment to excellence in football

Our Story

TRFC was founded by local parents, former athletes, and coaches who saw a need for meaningful change in our region's youth soccer landscape. In a city of over 150,000 residents served by a single soccer organization, we emerged as an alternative — built from the ground up by the community, for the community.

At TRFC, we provide youth athletes with high-quality, competitive soccer training rooted in strong character development, sportsmanship, and purpose. We believe that soccer is more than just a game — it is a powerful platform to shape young lives physically, mentally, and spiritually.

Our Core Values

TRFC is founded on an inclusive, player-centered environment where honesty, integrity, discipline, teamwork, and leadership are modeled and expected. While we welcome all backgrounds, our club is proudly guided by Christian values and led with a Christ-first approach — ensuring that our actions on and off the field reflect humility, service, and love.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in training, performance, and personal development, pushing our players to reach their full potential.

Community

Soccer is more than a game - it's about bringing people together. We're proud to be an integral part of our local community.

Passion

Our love for the game drives everything we do. This passion is what makes TRFC special and keeps us moving forward.
