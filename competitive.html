ABOUT OUR COMPETITIVE ACADEMY

The Texarkana Rovers Competitive Academy bridges the gap between our Kit Academy youth program and our elite Rovers FC teams. We provide a professional training environment for players ages U10-U16 who are serious about soccer development and competitive play.

Our academy combines the technical foundation from Kit Academy with the competitive intensity of Rovers FC, creating a complete pathway for player development. We focus on advanced technical skills, tactical understanding, physical conditioning, and mental preparation for competitive soccer.
